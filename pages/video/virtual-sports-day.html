Virtual Sports Day

During the Covid-19 Pandemic, sports day had to be done a bit differently. People did their own sports at home, and recorded what they did on tracking apps. The recordings were sent in and distances totalled up to try and reach the length from Wells to Olympia.


I took everyone's .GPX files from their tracking app and plotted them onto Google Earth, so I could do 'fly overs' highlighting the route. This was by far the quickest project I had done to date, with everything being done within 24 hours.
